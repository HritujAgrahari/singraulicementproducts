Key Facilities & Equipment

⚙

Concrete Mixing Plant

Calibrated concrete mixing equipment ensures precise mix proportions and uniform consistency across all production batches.

🛠

Hydraulic / Vibration Press

Modern block-making machines with hydraulic or vibratory compaction for dense, uniform products with accurate dimensions.

📅

Curing Yard

Dedicated curing area with moisture retention facilities to ensure products achieve full design strength over the standard 28-day curing period.

📊

Quality Testing Lab

In-house testing for compressive strength, dimensions, water absorption and surface finish before product approval and dispatch.

📦

Raw Material Storage

Covered storage for cement and aggregate stockpiles — protecting raw materials from moisture and contamination to maintain product quality.

🚚

Dispatch & Logistics

Well-organised dispatch yard for bulk loading and efficient delivery to construction sites across Sidhi, Singrauli and surrounding districts.
